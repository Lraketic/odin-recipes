Cevapi

Description

Cevapi is a grilled dish of minced meat found traditionally in the countries of southeast Europe (the Balkans).

It is considered a national dish of Bosnia and Herzegovina and Serbia and is also common in Croatia, Montenegro, Kosovo, North Macedonia and Slovenia.

Ingredients

  • Ground meat - Beef is ok but you can use whatever you have
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Bread or Somun

Steps

  1. Mix ground meat with garlic, Salt, Pepper.
  2. Using your hands, make small cylindrical shaped cevapcici, just like on the image above.
  3. Place cevapcici on hot well oiled pan or a grill.
  4. Take them off the heat once they go brown, and serve with bread/somun with diced onions.
  5. Enjoy!
